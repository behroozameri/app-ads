Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Android and iOS versions of the mobile application Color Memory - Glow Sequence, published by BPZone.

Information We Do Not Collect Directly

Color Memory - Glow Sequence does not require users to create an account. We do not directly collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, physical address, payment information, health information, contacts, photos, videos, audio recordings, precise location, or other sensitive personal information.

Information Collected Automatically

The app and the third-party services used in the app may automatically collect or process device-related, usage, advertising, consent, analytics, and diagnostics information. This may include:

  • Approximate location inferred from IP address
  • Device identifiers, such as advertising identifiers or app/device-scoped identifiers
  • App interactions, such as app launches, taps, game events, screen views, and ad interactions
  • Advertising data, such as ads shown, ad impressions, ad clicks, and rewarded ad events
  • Diagnostics and performance data, such as crash or error logs, launch time, hang rate, and energy usage
  • Consent choices and privacy preferences
  • Android notification tokens when push notifications are enabled or available

Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services that may collect, process, or share information to provide ads, analytics, consent management, diagnostics, messaging, security, fraud prevention, and service improvement. These services may include:

  • Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads SDK on Android and iOS
  • Google User Messaging Platform / AdMob Privacy & Messaging on Android and iOS
  • Firebase Analytics on Android and iOS
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging on Android only
  • Google Play Services on Android

Please review the privacy policies and information pages of these third-party service providers:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy and Security
  • Google Mobile Ads SDK Android Data Disclosure
  • Google Mobile Ads SDK iOS Data Disclosure
  • Google AdMob Privacy & Messaging

Advertising

Color Memory - Glow Sequence displays ads using Google AdMob. Ads may include banner ads, interstitial ads shown after game over screens, and rewarded ads. Rewarded ads are optional and may be shown when users choose to receive in-app benefits such as extra coins or the ability to watch a color sequence again.

Google AdMob may use device identifiers, approximate location, advertising data, app interaction data, diagnostics, and similar technologies to provide, measure, secure, and improve ads. Depending on your platform settings, consent choices, and region, ads may be personalized, non-personalized, limited, or otherwise restricted.

Analytics

The app uses Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the app and to improve gameplay, performance, and user experience. Analytics data may include events such as screen views, game starts, level progress, wrong answers, game over events, rewarded ad interactions, theme selections, settings changes, and other app usage events.

Consent and Privacy Choices

In regions where consent or privacy choices are required, such as the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, Switzerland, and certain U.S. states, the app may display privacy or consent messages using Google User Messaging Platform and AdMob Privacy & Messaging.

Users may be able to manage or change their ad privacy choices from within the app by opening Settings and selecting Privacy Choices, when this option is required or available for their region.

Push Notifications

The Android version of Color Memory - Glow Sequence may use Firebase Cloud Messaging to send push notifications, such as app updates, gameplay reminders, rewards, or other app-related messages. On supported Android versions, the app may ask for notification permission before notifications are shown.

Firebase Cloud Messaging may process a notification token and device-related information to deliver notifications. We do not use push notifications to send sensitive personal information. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

The iOS version of the app does not currently use Firebase Cloud Messaging or request permission to send push notifications.

In-App Data Stored on Your Device

The app may store gameplay progress locally on your device, including coins, streak, best scores, best levels, selected theme, sound settings, ad cooldown state, and other gameplay preferences. This information is used to provide the game experience and is stored on your device. You can remove locally stored data by clearing the app data or uninstalling the app, depending on your device platform.

Your Choices

You can manage certain privacy choices through the app settings when available. You can also use your device settings to reset or limit advertising identifiers, manage app tracking permissions on iOS where applicable, control Android notification permission, or uninstall the app.

Children's Privacy

Color Memory - Glow Sequence is a general audience memory game and is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Data Security
