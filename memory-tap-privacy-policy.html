Information We Collect

Memory Tap Challenge does not require users to create an account and does not directly collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or address.

The app may collect non-personal usage information automatically through third-party services, such as app interactions, gameplay events, device information, diagnostics, advertising identifiers, and analytics data.

The app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These services include:

Advertising

Memory Tap Challenge displays ads using Google AdMob. Ads may include banner ads, interstitial ads, and rewarded ads. Rewarded ads are optional and may be shown when users choose to receive in-app rewards such as coins, hints, or reveal assistance.

Google AdMob may use advertising identifiers and similar technologies to provide, measure, and improve ads. Depending on your device settings and region, you may be able to limit ad personalization through your device settings.

Analytics

The app uses Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the app and to improve gameplay, performance, and user experience. Analytics data may include events such as game mode starts, level completions, level failures, hint usage, rewarded ad interactions, and other app usage events.

In-App Data

The app may store gameplay progress locally on your device, including levels, coins, best score, selected theme, and settings. This information is used only to provide the game experience and is stored on your device.

Children's Privacy

Memory Tap Challenge is designed as a general audience brain training game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.
