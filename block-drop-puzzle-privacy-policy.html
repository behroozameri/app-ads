Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application Block Drop Puzzle published by BPZone.

Information We Collect

Block Drop Puzzle does not require users to create an account and does not directly collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or address.

The app may collect or process non-personal and device-related information automatically through third-party services, such as app interactions, game events, device information, diagnostics, advertising identifiers, notification tokens, consent choices, and analytics data.

Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services that may collect information used to provide, improve, measure, personalize, or secure their services. These services include:

  • Google AdMob
  • Google User Messaging Platform / AdMob Privacy & Messaging
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging
  • Google Play Services

Please review the privacy policies and information pages of these third-party service providers:

Advertising

Block Drop Puzzle displays ads using Google AdMob. Ads may include banner ads, interstitial ads, and rewarded ads.

Banner ads may be shown on non-gameplay screens, such as the home or settings screens. Interstitial ads may be shown at natural transition points, such as after a game round ends. Rewarded ads are optional and may be shown when users choose to continue after game over or earn an in-game bomb reward.

Google AdMob may use advertising identifiers, cookies or similar technologies, device information, app interactions, diagnostics, and approximate location derived from IP address to provide, measure, improve, personalize, and secure ads. Depending on your device settings, consent choices, and region, ads may be personalized or non-personalized.

Consent and Privacy Choices

In regions where consent is required, such as the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland, the app may display a consent message using Google User Messaging Platform and AdMob Privacy & Messaging before ads are requested.

Users may be able to manage or change their ad privacy choices from within the app by opening Settings and selecting Privacy Choices, when this option is required or available for their region.

Push Notifications

Block Drop Puzzle may use Firebase Cloud Messaging to send push notifications, such as app updates, gameplay reminders, or other app-related messages. On supported Android versions, the app may ask for notification permission before notifications are shown.

Firebase Cloud Messaging may process a notification token and device-related information to deliver notifications. We do not use push notifications to send sensitive personal information. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

Analytics

The app uses Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the app and to improve gameplay, performance, and user experience. Analytics data may include events such as game starts, selected game modes, game over events, scores, rewarded ad interactions, and other app usage events.

In-App Data

The app may store gameplay information locally on your device, including high score, total cleared lines, games played, best line count, selected game mode, and other app preferences. This information is used to provide the game experience and is stored on your device.

Children's Privacy

Block Drop Puzzle is designed as a general audience puzzle game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.
