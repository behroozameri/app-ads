Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile game published by BPZone, including Block Drop Quest on iOS and Block Drop Puzzle on Android. In this policy, these versions are referred to together as the App.

Information We Collect

The App does not require users to create an account and does not directly collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or mailing address.

The App may collect or process non-personal, device-related, and usage-related information automatically through third-party services. This may include app interactions, game events, device information, diagnostics, advertising identifiers, consent choices, approximate location derived from IP address, and analytics data.

Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services that may collect information used to provide, improve, measure, personalize, or secure their services. Depending on the platform, these services may include:

  • Google AdMob for advertising on Android and iOS
  • Google User Messaging Platform / AdMob Privacy & Messaging for consent and privacy choices
  • Firebase Analytics for app analytics on Android and iOS
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging for push notifications in the Android version
  • Google Play Services in the Android version
  • Apple App Store services in the iOS version

Please review the privacy policies and information pages of these third-party service providers:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy and Security
  • Google AdMob Privacy Information
  • Google AdMob Privacy & Messaging
  • Apple Privacy Policy

Advertising

The App displays ads using Google AdMob. Ads may include banner ads, interstitial ads, and rewarded ads.

Banner ads may be shown on non-gameplay screens, such as the home or settings screens. Interstitial ads may be shown at natural transition points, such as after a game round ends. Rewarded ads are optional and may be shown when users choose to continue after game over or earn an in-game reward.

Google AdMob may use advertising identifiers, cookies or similar technologies, device information, app interactions, diagnostics, advertising data, and approximate location derived from IP address to provide, measure, improve, personalize, and secure ads. Depending on your device settings, consent choices, platform, and region, ads may be personalized or non-personalized.

On iOS, the App does not require users to create an account. If Apple App Tracking Transparency permission is required for a future advertising configuration, the App will request permission where required by Apple before accessing tracking identifiers.

Consent and Privacy Choices

In regions where consent is required, such as the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland, the App may display a consent message using Google User Messaging Platform and AdMob Privacy & Messaging before ads are requested.

Users may be able to manage or change their ad privacy choices from within the App by opening Settings and selecting Privacy Choices, when this option is required or available for their region.

Push Notifications

The Android version of the App may use Firebase Cloud Messaging to send push notifications, such as app updates, gameplay reminders, or other app-related messages. On supported Android versions, the App may ask for notification permission before notifications are shown.

Firebase Cloud Messaging may process a notification token and device-related information to deliver notifications. We do not use push notifications to send sensitive personal information. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

The iOS version does not currently use Firebase Cloud Messaging or push notifications.

Analytics

The App uses Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the App and to improve gameplay, performance, and user experience. Analytics data may include events such as game starts, selected game modes, game over events, scores, rewarded ad interactions, and other app usage events.

In-App Data

The App may store gameplay information locally on your device, including high score, total cleared lines, games played, best line count, selected game mode, and other app preferences. This information is used to provide the game experience and is stored on your device.

Children's Privacy

The App is designed as a general audience puzzle game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Data Security

We value your trust and use commercially reasonable means to protect information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Links to Other Sites

The App may contain links to external websites or app store pages, such as Google Play, the Apple App Store, developer pages, or third-party privacy policy pages. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of external websites.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
