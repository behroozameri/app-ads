Like most websites, technical information may be processed automatically by hosting, security, browser, or network systems when you visit the site. This website does not currently use advertising, analytics tracking, or account cookies. If that changes in the future, this policy will be updated to explain what is used and why.

The website links to external services, including Google Play, the Apple App Store, and individual app pages or privacy policy pages. Those external websites and app stores are governed by their own privacy practices. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of third-party websites.

Mobile App Privacy Policies

BPZone apps may use different services, such as ads, analytics, notifications, or app store services. Each app page links to the relevant app privacy policy where applicable. Please review the policy for the specific app you use.

Children's Privacy

This website is a general audience catalog. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through this website. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.
