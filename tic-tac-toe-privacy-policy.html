Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application Tic Tac Toe, also listed as Tic Tac Toe XO Challenge on some app stores, published by BPZone. This policy applies to both the Android and iOS versions of the app.

Information We Collect

Tic Tac Toe does not require users to create an account and does not directly collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or address.

The app may collect or process limited device-related and usage information automatically through third-party services. This may include coarse location derived from network information, device identifiers, app interactions, game events, diagnostics, advertising data, consent choices, and analytics data.

The app does not collect precise location, contacts, photos, videos, audio recordings, health data, financial information, browsing history, search history, or user-generated content.

Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services that may collect information used to provide, improve, measure, personalize, or secure their services. These services include:

  • Google AdMob
  • Google User Messaging Platform / AdMob Privacy & Messaging
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Google Play Services on Android

Please review the privacy policies and information pages of these third-party service providers:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy and Security
  • Google AdMob Privacy Information
  • Google AdMob Privacy & Messaging

Advertising

Tic Tac Toe displays ads using Google AdMob. Ads may include banner ads and interstitial ads.

Google AdMob may use advertising data, device identifiers, device information, coarse location, diagnostics, and similar technologies to provide, measure, and improve ads. Depending on your device settings, consent choices, operating system, and region, ads may be personalized or non-personalized.

The iOS version does not require users to grant App Tracking Transparency permission in order to use the app. The app is designed to show ads without requiring the user to allow tracking across other companies' apps and websites.

Consent and Privacy Choices

In regions where consent is required, such as the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland, the app may display a consent message using Google User Messaging Platform and AdMob Privacy & Messaging before ads are requested.

Users may be able to manage or change their ad privacy choices from within the app by opening Settings and selecting Privacy Choices, when this option is required or available for their region.

Push Notifications

The app may use push notification services on supported platforms to send app-related messages, such as app updates or gameplay reminders. If notifications are used, the app may ask for notification permission before notifications are shown.

Notification services may process a notification token and device-related information to deliver notifications. We do not use push notifications to send sensitive personal information. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

Analytics

The app uses Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the app and to improve gameplay, performance, and user experience. Analytics data may include events such as game starts, game modes, round completion, selected options, and other app usage events.

Diagnostics

Third-party services may collect diagnostics such as crash data, performance data, and other technical diagnostic data to help improve stability, performance, and the app experience.

In-App Data

The app may store gameplay information locally on your device, including wins, losses, draws, selected difficulty, selected board size, selected theme, and other app preferences. This information is used to provide the game experience and is stored on your device.

Children's Privacy

Tic Tac Toe is designed as a general audience board game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Data Security

We value your trust and use commercially reasonable means to protect information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Links to Other Sites

The app may contain links to external websites, such as Google Play, developer pages, or third-party privacy policy pages. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of external websites.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
