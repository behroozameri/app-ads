Privacy Policy

WhatsApp Stickers

Effective Date: June 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Android or iOS version of WhatsApp Stickers and related WhatsApp sticker apps published by BPZone.

1) Scope

This policy applies to both the Android and iOS versions of the app.

The app is a third-party sticker app for WhatsApp. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by WhatsApp or Meta.

2) Information We Collect Directly

We do not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, messages, or payment information.

The app may store small local settings on your device, such as sticker pack unlock status, ad frequency timing, or basic app preferences. These local settings are used to operate the app and are not sent by us to our own servers.

3) Sticker and WhatsApp Functionality

The app provides sticker packs that can be added to WhatsApp.

  • On Android, WhatsApp may read sticker pack files from the app through Android's sticker content provider flow.
  • On iOS, when you tap "Add to WhatsApp", the app temporarily places the sticker pack payload on the system pasteboard so WhatsApp can import it. This pasteboard item is used only for the sticker import flow and is configured to expire shortly.

We do not read your WhatsApp chats, WhatsApp contacts, or WhatsApp messages. WhatsApp's own handling of imported stickers is governed by WhatsApp/Meta's privacy policies.

4) Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services that may collect and process data independently according to their own privacy policies.

Google AdMob

The app uses Google AdMob to show ads, including banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads. AdMob may collect information such as:

  • Device information, such as device model, operating system, language, and network details
  • Advertising identifiers, such as Android Advertising ID or Apple's IDFA where available and permitted
  • IP address and approximate location derived from IP address
  • Ad interactions, such as ad views, clicks, rewarded ad completion, and related advertising data
  • Diagnostic, crash, and performance information related to ads and SDK operation

AdMob may use this information to provide ads, measure ad performance, prevent fraud and abuse, improve services, and, where permitted, personalize ads.

Learn more: Google Privacy Policy, Google Advertising, and How Google uses information from apps and sites that use Google services.

Google User Messaging Platform

The app may use Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request and manage privacy choices where required, such as consent for personalized ads or privacy options in supported regions. If privacy choices are required, the app may show a "Privacy Choices" option in the app's information/settings screen.

Firebase Analytics

The Android and iOS versions may use Firebase Analytics to understand general app usage and improve the app. Firebase Analytics may collect data such as:

  • App opens and basic app usage events
  • Sticker pack interactions, such as opening a pack or tapping "Add to WhatsApp"
  • Rewarded ad unlock events
  • App instance identifiers and device/app information used for analytics

We use analytics in aggregated form to understand app performance, feature usage, and audience behavior. We do not use Firebase Analytics to collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, contacts, or WhatsApp messages.

Learn more: Firebase Privacy and Security and Firebase data collection.

Firebase Cloud Messaging on Android

The Android version may use Firebase Cloud Messaging to receive app notifications, if notifications are enabled. Firebase may generate a registration token for notification delivery. The iOS version of this app does not use push notifications or Firebase Messaging.

5) Tracking and Advertising Choices

Advertising and analytics partners may use identifiers to measure ads or, where allowed, personalize advertising.

  • Android: You can reset or delete your Android Advertising ID and manage ad personalization in your device's Google or privacy settings.
  • iOS: Where required, the app may ask for permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework before allowing tracking across apps and websites owned by other companies. You can change this choice in iOS Settings.
  • Ad consent: In supported regions, the app may show Google privacy or consent forms and may provide a "Privacy Choices" option.

If you decline personalized advertising or tracking where applicable, the app may still show non-personalized or contextual ads.

6) App Permissions

The app requests only permissions needed for app functionality, advertising, analytics, and optional notifications where applicable. You can manage app permissions in your device settings.

7) Data Sharing

We may share data with third-party service providers only as needed to operate, analyze, and monetize the app. These providers include Google services such as AdMob, Firebase Analytics, Firebase Cloud Messaging on Android, and Google User Messaging Platform. These providers process data according to their own policies and applicable laws.

We do not sell your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, WhatsApp messages, or WhatsApp chats because the app does not collect those items from you.

8) Children and Families

The app is intended for a general audience and is not specifically directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete such information.

9) Data Security

We do not operate our own account system or store sensitive personal data on our own servers. Third-party providers such as Google implement their own security measures for data they process. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we aim to use reputable service providers and limit the data collected by the app.

10) Data Retention

Local app settings remain on your device until you delete the app or clear app data. Data processed by third-party services is retained according to those providers' policies and your privacy choices.

11) International Data Transfers

Third-party services may process information on servers located outside your country or region. By using the app, you understand that information may be processed in countries with different data protection laws than your own.

12) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above and publishing the updated policy at the same privacy policy URL.
