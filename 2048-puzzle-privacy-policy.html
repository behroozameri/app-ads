Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Android and iOS versions of the mobile application 2048, published by BPZone.

Information We Collect

2048 does not require users to create an account and does not directly collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or address.

The app may collect or process non-personal and device-related information automatically through third-party services, such as app interactions, game events, device information, diagnostics, advertising identifiers, consent choices, and analytics data.

Platform Availability

2048 is available for Android through Google Play and for iOS through the Apple App Store. Store providers and operating system providers may process information related to downloads, updates, ratings, reviews, crash reports, device settings, and store account activity according to their own privacy policies.

Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services that may collect information used to provide, improve, measure, personalize, or secure their services. These services may include:

  • Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads
  • Google User Messaging Platform / AdMob Privacy & Messaging
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Google Play Services, on Android
  • Apple App Store and iOS platform services, on iOS

Please review the privacy policies and information pages of these third-party service providers:

  • Google Privacy Policy

  • Google AdMob Privacy Information

  • Apple App Store & Privacy

Advertising

2048 displays ads using Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads on Android and iOS. Ads may include banner ads and rewarded ads. Rewarded ads are optional and may be shown when users choose to receive extra undo credits or continue/rescue a game after game over.

Google AdMob may use advertising identifiers, device information, app activity, diagnostics, cookies, and similar technologies to provide, measure, and improve ads. Depending on your device settings, consent choices, platform rules, and region, ads may be personalized or non-personalized.

On Android, advertising-related processing may use Google Play Services and Android advertising identifiers where available. On iOS, advertising-related processing may use Apple privacy features such as SKAdNetwork and iOS advertising identifiers where available and permitted by device settings and platform rules.

Consent and Privacy Choices

In regions where consent is required, such as the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland, the app may display a consent message using Google User Messaging Platform and AdMob Privacy & Messaging before ads are requested.

Users may be able to manage or change their ad privacy choices from within the app by opening Settings and selecting Privacy Choices, when this option is required or available for their region.

Users can also control some advertising and privacy settings at the device or platform level, such as Android advertising ID settings, Google account ad settings, iOS tracking and advertising settings, and App Store privacy settings.

Analytics

The app uses Firebase Analytics on Android and iOS to understand how users interact with the app and to improve gameplay, performance, and user experience. Analytics data may include events such as app opens, new games, best score updates, game over summaries, undo usage, rewarded ad interactions, banner ad events, and other app usage events.

In-App Data

The app may store gameplay information locally on your device, including best score, current game state, undo credits, sound preferences, rewarded ad cooldown state, and app preferences. This information is used to provide the game experience and is stored on your device using Android or iOS local storage.

Local gameplay data may be removed by clearing the app data, deleting the app, or using operating system storage controls, depending on your device.

Children's Privacy

2048 is designed as a general audience puzzle game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.
