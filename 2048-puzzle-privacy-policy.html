Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application 2048 published by BPZone.

Information We Collect

2048 does not require users to create an account and does not directly collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or address.

The app may collect or process non-personal and device-related information automatically through third-party services, such as app interactions, game events, device information, diagnostics, advertising identifiers, consent choices, and analytics data.

Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services that may collect information used to provide, improve, measure, personalize, or secure their services. These services include:

  • Google AdMob
  • Google User Messaging Platform / AdMob Privacy & Messaging
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Google Play Services

Please review the privacy policies and information pages of these third-party service providers:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy and Security
  • Google AdMob Privacy Information
  • Google AdMob Privacy & Messaging

Advertising

2048 displays ads using Google AdMob. Ads may include banner ads and rewarded ads. Rewarded ads are optional and may be shown when users choose to receive extra undo credits by selecting the Get +5 Undo option.

Google AdMob may use advertising identifiers, cookies, device information, and similar technologies to provide, measure, and improve ads. Depending on your device settings, consent choices, and region, ads may be personalized or non-personalized.

Consent and Privacy Choices

In regions where consent is required, such as the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland, the app may display a consent message using Google User Messaging Platform and AdMob Privacy & Messaging before ads are requested.

Users may be able to manage or change their ad privacy choices from within the app by opening Settings and selecting Privacy Choices, when this option is required or available for their region.

Analytics

The app uses Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the app and to improve gameplay, performance, and user experience. Analytics data may include events such as app opens, new games, undo usage, rewarded ad interactions, banner ad events, and other app usage events.

In-App Data

The app may store gameplay information locally on your device, including best score, current game state, undo credits, and app preferences. This information is used to provide the game experience and is stored on your device.

Children's Privacy

2048 is designed as a general audience puzzle game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.
