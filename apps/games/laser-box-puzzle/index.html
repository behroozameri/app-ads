Laser Box Puzzle

Guide laser beams through mirrors, glass splitters, checkpoints, and receivers in compact logic circuits built for thoughtful puzzle sessions.

Available for Android and iOS

Optics puzzles with instant beam feedback

Every move changes the path of light, so each circuit becomes a small spatial logic problem.

MI
Movable mirrors

Slide and rotate optical boxes to bend the laser around corners and toward the receiver.

GL
Glass splitters

Use glass to continue the beam forward while creating a reflected branch.

CP
Checkpoint circuits

Complete a level by lighting every checkpoint and at least one receiver.

3S
Three-star scoring

Solve efficiently, beat the par, and keep your best stars saved locally.

Six tutorials, then endless generated levels

The opening experiments teach reflection, splitting, rotation, walls, and checkpoints before the game expands into deterministic generated circuits.

Handcrafted tutorials

Start with six guided circuits that introduce each optical rule one step at a time.

Stable generated puzzles

Later levels are generated from known solved layouts, so each level stays consistent when you return.

Undo and restart

Experiment freely with undo history, restart, and extra mirrors when you want another route.

Move the boxes and bend the beam

Small boards, clear controls, live laser paths, and readable puzzle states.
