Word Voyage

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 21, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Word Voyage (bundle identifier com.bpzone.ca.wordvoyage) handles information when you use the app. Word Voyage is provided by BPZone (“we,” “us,” or “our”).

Word Voyage does not require an account. We do not directly request your name, email address, contacts, photos, precise location, or payment information.

2. Information stored on your device

The app stores the following game information locally on your device:

  • Completed levels and unlocked progress;
  • Stars and coin balance;
  • Hint usage and advertising frequency controls;
  • Haptic feedback preferences; and
  • Unfinished Knot Logic puzzle drafts.

This information is used only to operate the game and remember your progress. It is not uploaded to an account or a BPZone server. You can remove it by using Reset Progress in the app or by deleting the app.

3. Advertising

Word Voyage uses Google AdMob to display optional rewarded ads and occasional interstitial ads. Rewarded ads are shown only after you choose to watch one in exchange for an in-game coin reward.

The Google Mobile Ads SDK may automatically collect or process information such as:

  • IP address and approximate general location;
  • Device, app, or advertising identifiers available under your device settings;
  • Ads viewed and interactions with ads;
  • App interactions related to advertising;
  • Performance information; and
  • Crash and diagnostic information.

Google may use this information to provide and measure advertising, prevent fraud and abuse, maintain its services, and improve advertising performance. Google acts according to its own privacy policies and your applicable consent and device settings.

Learn more at How Google uses information from apps that use its services and in the Google Privacy Policy .

4. Consent and advertising choices

Where required by law, Word Voyage uses Google’s User Messaging Platform to request advertising privacy choices before requesting ads. If privacy options are required for your region, a Privacy Choices option is available in the app’s Settings screen.

You can also review Google advertising controls through Google Ad Settings and manage privacy permissions in your iOS device settings.

5. How information is shared

We do not sell personal information. Information may be processed by Google in connection with the advertising services described above. We may also disclose information if required by law, legal process, or to protect users, the app, or our rights.

6. Data retention

Local game information remains on your device until you reset your progress or delete the app. Information processed by Google is retained according to Google’s own policies and the privacy choices available to you.

7. Children’s privacy

Word Voyage is a general-audience word puzzle game. We do not knowingly ask children to provide personal information directly to us. Parents and guardians should supervise children’s use of the app and review the advertising and device privacy controls available in their region.

If you believe a child has provided personal information in connection with the app, please contact us using the method described below.

8. International processing

Google and its service providers may process advertising information in countries other than the country where you live. Data protection laws in those countries may differ from those in your jurisdiction.

9. Security

We use reasonable measures to protect the information handled by the app. However, no storage or transmission method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

10. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have privacy rights relating to access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, or withdrawal of consent. Because the app’s game progress is stored locally, you can delete that information using Reset Progress or by deleting the app.

Requests concerning information processed directly by Google can be submitted through Google’s privacy controls and policies linked above.

11. External links

The app may open external services such as the Apple App Store or advertising content. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app, its advertising services, or legal requirements change. The updated policy will be published at this location with a revised effective date.

13. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact BPZone through the Support link on the Word Voyage App Store product page. Please include “Word Voyage Privacy” in your message.
