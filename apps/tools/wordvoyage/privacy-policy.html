Word Voyage

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 21, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Word Voyage (bundle identifier com.bpzone.ca.wordvoyage) handles information when you use the app. Word Voyage is provided by BPZone (“we,” “us,” or “our”).

Word Voyage does not require an account. We do not directly request your name, email address, contacts, photos, precise location, or payment information.

2. Information stored on your device

The app stores the following game information locally on your device:

  • Completed levels and unlocked progress;
  • Stars and puzzle progress;
  • Hint usage;
  • Haptic feedback preferences; and
  • Unfinished Knot Logic puzzle drafts.

This information is used only to operate the game and remember your progress. It is not uploaded to an account or a BPZone server. You can remove it by using Reset Progress in the app or by deleting the app.

3. Usage analytics

Word Voyage uses Google Analytics for Firebase to understand how the app is used and improve future versions. Analytics may process information such as:

  • App launches and general screen or game-mode usage;
  • Level starts, completions, difficulty, stars, and the number of hints used;
  • App instance and device information; and
  • General performance and diagnostic information.

We do not send puzzle words, selected letters, typed content, your name, email address, or a custom user ID to Firebase Analytics.

You can turn analytics collection on or off at any time using Share Usage Analytics in the app’s Settings screen. Turning it off stops future analytics event collection from the app on that device.

Firebase Analytics is provided by Google. Learn more in Firebase Privacy and Security .

4. Advertising

The current version of Word Voyage does not request, load, or display advertising. Google AdMob is not active in this release.

A future version may introduce optional advertising. Before advertising is enabled, this Privacy Policy and the app’s App Store privacy information will be updated to describe the applicable data practices and privacy choices.

5. Consent and advertising choices

Because advertising is not active in the current release, the app does not present an advertising consent form or advertising privacy controls.

6. How information is shared

We do not sell personal information. Information may be processed by Google in connection with the analytics service described above. We may also disclose information if required by law, legal process, or to protect users, the app, or our rights.

7. Data retention

Local game information remains on your device until you reset your progress or delete the app. Analytics information processed by Google is retained according to Google’s own policies and the privacy choices available to you.

8. Children’s privacy

Word Voyage is a general-audience word puzzle game. We do not knowingly ask children to provide personal information directly to us. Parents and guardians should supervise children’s use of the app and review the device privacy controls available in their region.

If you believe a child has provided personal information in connection with the app, please contact us using the method described below.

9. International processing

Google and its service providers may process analytics information in countries other than the country where you live. Data protection laws in those countries may differ from those in your jurisdiction.

10. Security

We use reasonable measures to protect the information handled by the app. However, no storage or transmission method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

11. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have privacy rights relating to access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, or withdrawal of consent. Because the app’s game progress is stored locally, you can delete that information using Reset Progress or by deleting the app.

Requests concerning information processed directly by Google can be submitted through Google’s privacy controls and policies linked above.

12. External links

The app may open external services such as the Apple App Store. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app, its services, or legal requirements change. The updated policy will be published at this location with a revised effective date.

14. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, visit BPZone Support or email bpzone.ca@gmail.com. Please include “Word Voyage Privacy” in your message.
