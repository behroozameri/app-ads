Thread Circle by BPZone

Privacy Policy

Effective and last updated: June 15, 2026

Thread Circle processes your selected photos entirely on your device. BPZone does not collect, upload, store, sell, or share your personal data.

1. About This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to Version 1 of the iOS application Thread Circle by BPZone, also displayed on your device as Thread Circle. The app is developed by Behrooz Ameri and published under the BPZone name.

2. Data We Collect

BPZone does not collect personal data through the app. In particular:

  • No account or registration is required.
  • No analytics or behavioral tracking is used.
  • No advertising SDK is included in Version 1.
  • No location, contacts, identifiers, or usage history is collected.
  • No photo or generated artwork is sent to BPZone or any app server.

3. Photos and On-Device Processing

The app lets you choose a photo from your device to create a string art pattern. The selected image is processed locally on your iPhone or iPad. It is not uploaded to BPZone or processed on a remote server.

Photo access is used only to let you select the image you want to process. The app does not scan or copy your entire photo library.

4. Generated Files and Sharing

PNG and PDF exports are created locally and temporarily on your device. The app does not automatically upload these files.

If you choose to save or share an export using the iOS share sheet, the file is provided to the destination or third-party service you select. Apple or that service may process the file under its own privacy policy. BPZone does not control those external services.

5. Data Retention

BPZone has no server-side copy of your photos, patterns, or exports and therefore does not retain them. Version 1 does not save app projects between sessions. Temporary export files are managed locally by iOS, and files saved or shared by you remain wherever you choose to place them.

6. Third-Party Services

Version 1 does not integrate advertising, analytics, crash-reporting, social networking, cloud storage, or account services. Standard Apple system features, including the photo picker and share sheet, are governed by Apple's terms and privacy practices.

7. Children's Privacy

The app is not designed to collect information from anyone, including children. Because BPZone does not collect personal data through the app, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Security

Keeping photo processing on the device reduces exposure to network and server risks. However, no device or storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure. You are responsible for the destinations and services you choose when saving or sharing generated files.

9. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated if the app's features or data practices change. For example, a future version that introduces advertising or analytics will be covered by an updated policy before that version is released. The latest version will be published on the BPZone website with a revised effective date.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact Behrooz Ameri through the BPZone website: https://bpzone.info/
