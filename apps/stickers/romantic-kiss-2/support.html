Romantic Kiss Stickers Pack Support

Romantic Kiss Stickers Pack is a standalone iOS app for browsing, personalizing, copying, and sharing romantic stickers.

All 26 sticker collections are included with the app. No other app, account, sign-in, purchase, or advertisement is required to access them.

Browse and Search

Open the app to view all collections. Use the search field to find a collection by name, then tap a collection to browse its stickers.

Favorites

Open a sticker and choose Add to Favorites. Use the heart button on the main screen to view saved favorites. Favorites are stored locally on your device.

Personalize a Sticker

Open a sticker and choose Personalize Sticker. Add your own message, choose a text color, and then copy or share the finished greeting.

Copy or Share

Choose Copy to place a sticker on the iOS clipboard, or choose Share to use the standard iOS share sheet. You can also share a complete collection from its collection screen.

Troubleshooting

If the app is not behaving as expected, close and reopen it, confirm that you are using the latest App Store version, and restart your device. Sticker browsing and personalization work without a network connection.

Contact Support

Email bpzone.ca@gmail.com. Please include the app name, your iPhone model, iOS version, and a short description of the issue. We normally reply within two business days.
