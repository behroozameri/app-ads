Help

Common support topics

When contacting support, please include your device model, iOS or Android version, app version, and a short description of what happened.

Stickers will not open

Close and reopen the app, confirm that your device has an active internet connection if ads are being shown, and make sure you are using the latest app version.

Unlocking sticker packs

Some packs may require a rewarded ad. If an ad is not ready, locked packs may be hidden until an ad becomes available.

Sharing and copying

You can preview, copy, and share stickers from inside the app using the available sticker actions on your device.
