Privacy Policy

Romantic Kiss Stickers Pack

Effective Date: June 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and shared when you use the iOS version of Romantic Kiss Stickers Pack, published by BPZone.

1) Scope

This policy applies to the iOS app Romantic Kiss Stickers Pack.

The app is a standalone sticker browsing and sharing app. It does not require you to install another app, create an account, or sign in before browsing, copying, favoriting, personalizing, or sharing stickers.

2) Information We Collect Directly

We do not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, messages, or payment information.

The app may store small local settings on your device, such as your favorite stickers and basic app preferences. These local settings are used to operate the app and are not sent by us to our own servers.

3) Sticker Functionality

The app lets you browse and search sticker packs, save favorites, add custom text to create a personalized greeting, copy individual stickers, and share individual stickers or full sticker packs through the iOS share sheet.

Sticker images are included with the app or prepared locally on your device for sharing. The app does not read your contacts, messages, or personal photos.

4) Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services that may collect and process data independently according to their own privacy policies.

Google AdMob

The app uses Google AdMob to show non-blocking banner ads. Sticker content and app features do not depend on an ad loading. AdMob may collect information such as:

  • Device information, such as device model, operating system, language, and network details
  • Advertising identifiers, such as Apple's IDFA where available and permitted
  • IP address and approximate location derived from IP address
  • Ad interactions, such as banner ad views, clicks, and related advertising data
  • Diagnostic, crash, and performance information related to ads and SDK operation

AdMob may use this information to provide ads, measure ad performance, prevent fraud and abuse, improve services, and, where permitted, personalize ads.

Learn more: Google Privacy Policy, Google Advertising, and How Google uses information from apps and sites that use Google services.

Firebase Analytics

The iOS app may use Firebase Analytics to understand general app usage and improve the app. Firebase Analytics may collect data such as:

  • App opens and basic app usage events
  • Sticker pack interactions, such as opening a pack
  • App instance identifiers and device/app information used for analytics

We use analytics in aggregated form to understand app performance, feature usage, and audience behavior. We do not use Firebase Analytics to collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, contacts, or messages.

Learn more: Firebase Privacy and Security and Firebase data collection.

5) Tracking and Advertising Choices

Advertising and analytics partners may use identifiers to measure app usage and banner ad performance. This version does not request App Tracking Transparency permission.

Ad availability never limits access to stickers, favorites, personalization, copying, or sharing.

6) App Permissions

The app does not request access to contacts, messages, personal photos, the camera, microphone, or location. Copying and sharing use standard iOS system features.

7) Data Sharing

We may share data with third-party service providers only as needed to operate, analyze, and monetize the app. These providers include Google AdMob and Firebase Analytics. These providers process data according to their own policies and applicable laws.

We do not sell your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, or messages because the app does not collect those items from you.

8) Children and Families

The app is intended for a general audience and is not specifically directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete such information.

9) Data Security

We do not operate our own account system or store sensitive personal data on our own servers. Third-party providers such as Google implement their own security measures for data they process. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we aim to use reputable service providers and limit the data collected by the app.

10) Data Retention

Local app settings remain on your device until you delete the app or clear app data. Data processed by third-party services is retained according to those providers' policies and your privacy choices.

11) International Data Transfers

Third-party services may process information on servers located outside your country or region. By using the app, you understand that information may be processed in countries with different data protection laws than your own.

12) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above and publishing the updated policy at the same privacy policy URL.
